Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about finding your perfect roommate

Our platform uses a sophisticated matching algorithm that considers your preferences, lifestyle habits, and compatibility factors. You'll fill out a detailed profile, and we'll show you potential roommates who match your criteria. You can then browse profiles, connect with matches, and schedule meet-ups.

We take safety seriously. All users must verify their identity through a multi-step process including email verification, phone number verification, and optional social media linking. We also encourage users to meet in public places first and follow our safety guidelines when meeting potential roommates.

Basic profile creation and browsing is free. Premium features, such as unlimited messaging and advanced filters, are available through our subscription plans. We also offer a secure payment system for handling rent and utility payments once you've found your roommate.

You can list your existing space and specify that you're looking for someone to fill a room. Make sure to include details about the apartment, rent, utilities, and any house rules or preferences you have for potential roommates.

Once you've found a potential match, you can use our built-in messaging system to communicate. We recommend starting with platform messages before sharing personal contact information. This helps maintain privacy and safety for all users.

If you experience any issues, our support team is here to help. You can contact us 24/7 through our help center, and we'll assist with conflict resolution, payment disputes, or any other concerns. We also provide resources and guidelines for handling common roommate situations.
